## 1. Privacy at a Glance

### General Information

The following information provides a simple overview of what happens to your personal data when you visit this website. Personal data is any data that can be used to personally identify you.

### Data Collection on This Website

Data processing on this website is carried out by the project participants of Akku-Craft (see [Legal Notice/Impressum](/legal/imprint)).

## 2. Hosting and Content Delivery Networks (CDN)

### Vercel

We host this website with Vercel (Vercel Inc., 340 S Lemon Ave #4133, Walnut, CA 91789, USA).

Vercel collects technically necessary data (log files), such as your IP address, to ensure the website is displayed stably and securely. Data transfer to the USA is secured by standard contractual clauses of the EU Commission.

Legal basis: Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest in error-free presentation).

### Cloudflare

We use Cloudflare (Cloudflare Inc., 101 Townsend St., San Francisco, CA 94107, USA) as a nameserver service. Requests are technically routed through Cloudflare infrastructure. Cloudflare processes IP addresses for security and performance optimization.

## 3. General Notes and Mandatory Information

### Your Rights

You have the right at any time to receive information free of charge about the origin, recipient, and purpose of your stored personal data. You also have a right to request correction or deletion of this data. To do so, please contact the email address provided in the Legal Notice (Impressum).

## 4. Data Collection on This Website

### Contact Form / Email

If you send inquiries via the contact form or email, your details (name, email, message) will be stored for the purpose of processing the inquiry. We use iCloud Mail (Apple) for this. We do not share this data without your consent.

### Cookies

We only use technically necessary cookies. These are used to store your settings for design (light/dark mode). These cookies are not used for advertising or tracking purposes.

### Vercel Web Analytics

We use Vercel Analytics to analyze visitor statistics. This helps us better understand and optimize the use of this website. Vercel Analytics is configured so that no cookies are stored in your browser. IP addresses are anonymized immediately upon collection, so we cannot draw conclusions about your identity.

Legal basis: Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest in statistical analysis to optimize this website).

### Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts via the next/font/google package. Fonts are integrated locally into this project. This means no connection is established to Google servers when loading the website, and no IP addresses are transmitted to Google.

### GitHub API

We integrate statistics via the GitHub API. Calls are made server-side, so your IP address is not directly transmitted to GitHub when visiting this website.

## 5. External Links

This website contains links to external platforms such as GitHub. If you click these links, you leave this website and the privacy policy of the respective provider applies.
